Sinigang

Pork Belly Sinigang sa Sampaloc is a shared recipe from Agnes. She writes: Sinigang is very good during the cold season.
No need for many vegetables, as long as there is kangkong, gabi and liempo. I suggest that you prepare patis and crushed hot pepper for the dipping sauce. Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo sliced liempo pork belly
  • 1 bundle chopped kangkong
  • 3 peeled taro roots gabi
  • 1/4 kilo fresh unripe tamarind
  • 6 cups tap water
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 2 chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons patis
  • 1 long green pepper

Instructions:

  1. Put the water and onion, tomato, and tamarind in a casserole. Let it boil for 20 minutes.
  2. Remove the tamarind.
  3. Mix the pork belly in the boiling water and cook it for 45 minutes.
  4. Add the taro root and pepper. Cook it for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the kangkong and patis. Cook it for 2 minutes.
  6. Serve this with rice.
